About

Incoroporating technology in the classroom can be a huge task for a teacher. Finding resources, teaching students how to use the technology, and sometimes learning how to use the technology yourself as the teacher are many reasons some shy away from new technology.

However, with proper prep and resources, tech can change your classroom! Technology can increase engagement in your classroom, and help your students interact more with their peers. Technology can also help students show their creativity in new ways!

This website was created to provide teachers with the benefits and challenges of incorporating technology in the classroom, as well as provide tools to help you get started.
