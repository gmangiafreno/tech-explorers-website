USEFUL APPS TO TRY

These tools are user friendly and students will love them

TECHNOLOGY THAT CREATES A FUN LEARNING ENVIRONMENT

Elementary school students today have been surrounded by technology their whole lives. They are curious, and want to learn as much as they can. These apps will help you keep your student's inquisitive minds thinking and learning while they have fun. These apps can be used with one-to-one devices, or at a technology center in your classroom.

EDPUZZLE

Empower students to take an active role in their learning with interactive video lessons that spark creativity and curiosity

NEARPOD

Real-time insights into student understanding through interactive lessons, interactive videos, gamification, and activities

BLOOKET

Students are encouraged to participate in games with rewards for answering questions and exploring new methods of learning

KEYNOTE

Bring your lesson to life with cinematic animations and transitions that look as though they were created by your own special effects team

FLIP

Use the simple and fun built-in camera to record your thoughts, add effects and share within your Flip group as video, audio only or text in your web browser or mobile app

GOOGLE CLASSROOM

Helps educators create engaging learning experiences they can personalize, manage, and measure
